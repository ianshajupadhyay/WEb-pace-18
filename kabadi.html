Kabadi RULES RULES

KABADDI

Contact: +917768092236

Entry Fee: Boys Rs.700
RULES:-
1.Each team must have minimum 10 and maximum 12 players. A playing squad of 7 shall take the
Ground at a time, and the remaining 3 or 5 are substitutes.
2.The duration of Match shall be a minimum of 30 minutes, divided equally into 2 halves of 15 minutes each, with a 5-minute internal between the two halves.
3.Scoring System
a) Each team will score one point for every opponent out or put out.
b) A player who scores ALL OUT (SUPER RAID) will be awarded with two extra points.
c) Each team shall score one point for every bonus point awarded.
4.Time Out
a) Each team is allowed to take 1 TIME OUT of 90 seconds per match.
b) During the time out, the match clock will be paused and will restart on commencement of next raid.
c) Official Time out can be called by referee in case of any injury.
5.Substitution
a) Each team is allowed a maximum number of 5 substitutions per match with the permission of referee.
b) Substitutions are not allowed for players who are out.
c) Players can be resubstituted.
d) Match clock will be officially stopped for the duration of substitution.
e) In case a team utilises all 5 substitutions and one of its players suffers an injury in the immediate raid after the final substitution, the referee may choose to allow substitutes.
6. Results
The team which scores highest number of points at the end of the match will be declared a winner.
7. In Case of tie
a) In case if tie, an extra time of 7 minutes will be awarded to decide the result of match.
b) This extra time of 7 minutes will be divided into halves of 3 minutes each and a break of 1 minute between the halves
c) The court and raid position will be the same as they were at the beginning of the match.
d) Regular rules of the play shall be followed.
8.If at the end of 7 minutes the game is still tied, 3 raid are given to each side and the winner will be declared accordingly.
